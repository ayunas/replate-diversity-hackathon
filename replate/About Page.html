rePlate

Why we need it

In 2017, an estimated 1 in 8 Americans were food insecure, equating to 40 million Americans including more than 12 million children. Children who experience food insecurity are at a higher risk of developing asthma, struggling with anxiety or depression, and performing poorly in school or physical activities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to feed the hungry by eliminating restaurant food waste. This empowers food establishments to support non profit organizations that aid people in need. Through this partnership we work to reduce food waste and help sustain people in need.

What we need

Restaurant: If you're associated with a Restaurant that commonly throws good food away, joining Replate can help you turn throw-away trash into life-saving meals. Our app helps you communicate with volunteer drivers.

Volunteers: Replate Volunteers transport food from restaurants to shelters and non profits that work with food insecure families. Our app helps you know exactly when, where, and how to make a successful food delivery.

Non Profits

Many non profit organizations work with food insecure families. Our Replate app helps connect you with Restaurants and Volunteers ready to help. Expand your impact by feeding more families!

This app is Free for both businesses and volunteer. All you need to do is fill out a special form to verify your restaurant business and non-profit organization and you are good to go.

App features

  1. User Friendly: Our app is so easy to navigate you will feel so smart.
  2. Always Updating: Our technology always keeps track of restaurants who are always wanting throw out food
  3. Good Karma: This app will help build a sense of community.
